Learning Objectives

  • Describe the fundamental properties of natural gas and its primary safety risks.
  • Identify common hazards in environments where natural gas is present.
  • Perform a basic risk assessment for tasks involving natural gas.
  • Select and use appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for gas-related work.
  • Execute correct emergency procedures in response to a natural gas leak or incident.
  • Understand the importance of and procedures for incident and near-miss reporting.
  • Recognize key safety regulations governing the natural gas industry.
  • Contribute to a culture of continuous safety improvement and personal accountability.
